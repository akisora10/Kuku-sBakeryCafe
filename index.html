About Us

Kuku's Bakery Cafe was established on 2020 during pandemic.
We only cater delivery services at first but due to public demands,
a physical store was built to serve customers firsthand.
Kuku's Bakery Cafe was owned by four sisters. We aspire to provide
a chill and cozy place for everyone while sipping and indulging on our homemade food and coffee.
